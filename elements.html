Mechanics of the Game

Step 1:

The class will be divided into 4 groups. Group 1 vs. Group 2 AND Group 3 vs. 4

Step 2

The Group who’ll raise their hands first will guess the answer to the riddles provided by the game masters.

Step 3

BUT, before answering the riddle, the WHOLE GROUP must be able to bring the things commanded by the game masters. These things have the same FIRST LETTER as the answer to the riddle.

Step 4

If the group was able to complete the things commanded to bring, their LEADER/ REPRESENTATIVE will click the RAISE HAND BUTTON.

Step 5

All members will show the items they got and so, IT IS NECESSARY TO ON THE CAMERA!

Step 6

The game masters will check if the items brought by the Group were right.

Step 7

Then, if it was right, the group can answer already their guess to the riddle. HOWEVER, if not, the chance to answer will be given to the opposing group.

Step 8

The group who’ll get 2 points first is QUALIFIED TO PLAY THE FINAL ROUND. This round will only last ONE TIME so better play it well.

Step 9

ALWAYS SHOW YOUR PRETTY AND HANDSOME FACES ON YOUR CAMERA:)
