Mechanics of the Game

Step 1:

The class will be divided into 4 groups. Group 1 vs. Group 2 AND Group 3 vs. Group 4

Step 2

Each group is required to answer their given riddle. For the elimination - round 1, group 1 will be the first to answer. While, for elimination - round 2, group 2 will be the first to answer.

Step 3

Game masters will set a stopwatch to observe how long it will take them to accomplish the command bring me and know the answer. The host will cite the riddle and will say "BRING IT ON" only then, you can start finding.

Step 4

BUT, before answering the riddle, their THREE REPRESENTATIVES must be able to bring the things in accordance with their answer. These things have the same FIRST LETTER as the answer to the riddle.

Step 5

Each representative should raise his/her hand if he/she is ready to show the thing and say the answer. Then, if we see that the three representatives are all prepared, they must show their respective things that represents their answer.

Step 6

All three representatives will show and say the items they got and so, IT IS NECESSARY TO STAY ON THE CAMERA. ^^

Step 7

The game masters will check if the items brought by the Group were right. Make sure that you brought the right representative item.

Step 8

Then, if the items and answers are connected to each other, they will be granted with 1 point. HOWEVER, if not, the chance to answer will be given to the opposing group.

Step 9

The group who’ll get 2 points first is QUALIFIED TO PLAY THE FINAL ROUND. This round will only last ONE TIME so better play it well. ;)

Step 10

The winner will receive 250 pesos! Remember: ALWAYS SHOW YOUR PRETTY AND HANDSOME FACES ON YOUR CAMERA:)

THINGS TO REMEMBER:

COOPERATION IS A MUST. Thank you, wonderful people.

It's important to stay focused and confident to ensure you and your team can answer questions correctly. Additionally, take note of your surroundings and become familiar with the items available to you, so you can quickly act when prompted by the game master. Lastly, trust in your team and enjoy the experience!

Flow of the Game

For example:

Group 1 vs. Group 2 and Group 3 vs. Group 4 ang maglalaban.
ROUND 1
Magbibigay po ng riddles ang game master.
Halimbawa: “Do not do to others what you do not want them to do to you” is our golden rule. And living a harmonious life is our goal. Xiao, the attitude of respect for elders is one of our core values.Yi, which means righteous and justice, is one of our basic virtues. What philosophy am I following? (ANSWER: CONFUCIANISM)
Hindi muna kayo agad sasagot. Kailangan munang makapagbring me ng 3 representative members ng group ng bagay na nagsisimula sa letter C ang pangalan. Unang member: "Charger for Confucianism". Second member: "Case for Confucianism". Third member: "Cellphone for Confucianism". Yung mga members na hindi representative, pwedeng sila ang maghanap ng sagot dito sa website para i-coach kayo." Kapag nakumpleto nyo na ang mga bagay at ready na kayong sumagot, magraise na ang mga members. SIGURADUHING KITA NAMIN SA CAMERA ANG NAKUHA NYONG BAGAY.^^ Kapag nakita naming kumpleto na kayong tatlo at naipakita na rin sa camera ang inyong bagay, saka po lamang namin ihihinto ang stopwatch.
Sasabihin ng host na pwede nyo nang imikin ang inyong sagot. Kapag tama ang inyong grupo, kailangang ma-beat ng Group 2 ang oras ninyo sa paghahanap. Halimbawa ang group 1 ay nakakuha ng 10 segundo, kinakailangan na hindi aabot o lalampas sa 10 segundo ang Group 2.
ROUND 2 - ELIMINATION ROUND
Same flow pa rin po ng nasa taas. Ang pagkakaiba lamang ay ang Group 2 naman ang mauunang magsagot ng riddle at ang Group 1 naman ang kailangang makabeat sa oras ng Group 2.
FINAL ROUND
Same flow pa rin po para sa kung sino mang dalawang group na mananalo.
There are possible answers above this section so pwede na po kayo magprepare ng mga bagay na nags-start sa letters na yun prior the game para ready na po ang lahat at madaling makakasagot ng riddles.
